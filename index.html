About Us

Coco Coopa Fried Chicken, founded in Oman in 2018 as Chicken City and rebranded in 2022, is renowned for its crispy fried chicken made from a secret family recipe. With 11 outlets and 15 more opening soon in Kerala and Karnataka, we aim to delight customers with delicious food and friendly service. Franchise opportunities are available for passionate entrepreneurs.
